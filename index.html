What is manga?

Manga is a Japanese comic book or graphic novel. It is a distinctive style of drawing and storytelling that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide. Manga covers a wide range of genres, from action and adventure stories to romance, horror, and sci-fi.

More info

In Japan, manga is usually read from right to left and often first appears as individual chapters in magazines before being compiled into volumes (called tankōbon). There are mangas for different age groups, such as shōnen (for young boys), shōjo (for girls), seinen (for adult men), and josei (for adult women). Manga has a significant influence on popular culture, and many popular anime series are based on manga originals.
